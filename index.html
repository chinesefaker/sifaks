Sifaks Ousmail is a linguist and researcher specialising in language policy and language education. With academic training spanning Paris, Cambridge, and Shanghai, Sifaks Ousmail brings an international perspective to the study of how languages are taught, governed, and contested across different social and institutional contexts.
