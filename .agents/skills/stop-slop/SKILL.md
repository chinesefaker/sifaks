# Stop Slop Skill

When writing or editing text, avoid generic AI-style writing.

## Avoid these patterns
- "It is worth noting that..."
- "In today's digital landscape..."
- "Unlock your potential..."
- "Seamlessly..."
- "Elevate..."
- "Dive into..."
- "Whether you're..."
- "Not only... but also..."
- Overuse of em dashes
- Overly polished marketing language
- Empty inspirational phrases
- Long sentences that sound artificial

## Writing style
- Write naturally and clearly.
- Use simple, direct language.
- Keep the user's original tone when editing.
- Prefer concrete wording over vague claims.
- Avoid sounding like a generic AI assistant.
- Do not over-explain obvious things.
- Keep public website text professional but human.

## For Sifaks's website
- Keep the tone academic, professional, minimal, and sincere.
- Avoid exaggerated self-promotion.
- Make research descriptions clear and credible.
- Use concise sentences.
- Keep SEO text natural, not keyword-stuffed.

## Before finishing
- Remove generic AI phrases.
- Check that the text sounds human.
- Explain what wording was changed and why.
